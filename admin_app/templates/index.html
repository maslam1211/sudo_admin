About SudoTag

SudoTag is a QR-based app designed to help users report various issues such as improper parking, unsafe practices, and more. Soon available on Play Store and App Store.

  • Report issues easily using a QR code scan.
  • Secure registration with personal details for better service.
  • Notify others instantly to resolve issues in real-time.

SudoTag provides a simple, effective, and secure way for users to report and resolve issues quickly. With an intuitive interface and real-time notifications, users can make their surroundings safer and more organized.

QR-Based Issue Reporting

Using SudoTag, you can instantly report issues by scanning a QR code. It's simple, fast, and effective.

Secure & Safe Registration

Sign up securely with your personal details like name and vehicle number. Your data is always kept safe and protected.

Easy App Integration

Our app integrates seamlessly with your mobile device, allowing you to register, report, and track issues in real-time.

Always Up-to-Date

SudoTag keeps the issue-reporting tags updated from the cloud, ensuring that all past reports are accessible and current.
